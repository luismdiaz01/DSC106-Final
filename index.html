The Homeless in San Diego

Density of Homeless Population By Block

Description:

In this map, the blocks in downtown San Diego are plotted with the density of the homeless people living in that area. By putting this on a map we can see which regions are very affected and how the problem affects san diego in different ways.

The Homeless Population By Neighborhood

Description:

Plotting the homeless population by the neighborhood gave us a way to see which areas were greatly affected. We can compare them over the course of a month and see which months were worse and if it was worse across all neighborhoods.

Table of Different Areas Where Homeless Sleep

Description:

Where people sleep when they are homeless is an important factor into trying to reduce the homeless population. People who have a shelter and structure to sleep ususally have more support and are able to get back on their feet. This table shows us the majority of homeless people still do not have shelters and sleep individually.
